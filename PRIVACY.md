# Privacy Policy

## Nightscout Monitor for Homey

Nightscout Monitor reads glucose-related data from the Nightscout URL configured by the user.

## Data processed

Depending on what is available in Nightscout, the app may process:

- Current glucose value
- Glucose trend
- Glucose delta
- Reservoir value
- Pump battery level
- CGM or uploader battery level
- Loop status
- Nightscout URL
- Nightscout API secret or token

## Data storage

The Nightscout URL and credential are stored locally on the user's Homey device as device settings/store values.

## Data sharing

The app does not send glucose data, credentials or Nightscout data to servers controlled by the developer.

Homey and Athom platform behavior is governed by Athom's own terms and privacy policies.

## External services

The app connects only to the Nightscout URL configured by the user.

## Medical disclaimer

This app is intended for home automation and informational display only. It is not a medical device and must not be used as the sole source for medical decisions, alarms or treatment.
