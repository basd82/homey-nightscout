# Nightscout Monitor

Display your Nightscout glucose data on Homey and use it in Flows.

## Features

- Current glucose value
- Trend direction
- Delta since previous reading
- Reservoir, pump battery, CGM/uploader battery and loop status when available in Nightscout
- mmol/L by default, optional mg/dL
- Connection test during pairing

## Examples

Use Homey Flows to change the color of a lamp based on your glucose value, or to send notifications for high or low readings.

## Note

This app is not a medical device. Do not use it as the sole source for medical decisions, alarms or treatment.
